HOW PREDICATE CAN HELP YOUR TEAM

Our support and customization model gives you access to expertise while ensuring you feel ownership over your instance.

Support Hours & Retainers

When you don't have time to spend hours digging through documentation or tinkering in a test environment, Predicate is here: jump on a screenshare or reach out to get the help you need.

Support hours are flexible and can be banked for when you need them.
Retainers include same day or next day call scheduling so you can see an expert without having to wait.

Reports, Dashboards, and Custom Portals

Drill down into your funnel, track event registrations and attendance, and compare the performance of email campaigns at the level your stakeholders need in order to make data-driven decisions.

We work both with Slate's native report builder and also develop custom dashboard portals when you need data presented in a different format.

We also know that just building the report or dashboard is the last step in the data journey: we work with you to identify data discrepancies, process gaps, and database performance optimizations to ensure your reports are truly accurate.

Branding Updates

Need help updating your Slate hosted forms, portals, and application pages to match a rebranded institutional website? We can help apply new styling and ensure you're compliant with institutional brand guidelines.

Training & Onboarding
